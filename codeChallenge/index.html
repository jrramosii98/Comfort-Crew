Our Mission



The Comfort Crew for Military Kids delivers proven resources to help military kids and their family connect and build resiliency in the face of extraordinary challenges.

Reach

The Comfort Crew for Military Kids reached their goal of serving over 20,000 military kids in 2019. Our resources and school assemblies were provided to military families living in 48 different states and overseas.
